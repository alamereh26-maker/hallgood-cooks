27 Dresses is a romantic comedy about Jane Nichols (Katherine Heigl), a selfless, perennial bridesmaid who has been in 27 weddings. While secretly in love with her boss, George, her life gets complicated when her manipulative younger sister, Tess, arrives, sweeps George off his feet, and enlists Jane to plan their wedding. Will she ever get to plan her own wedding? Go watch and find out!
